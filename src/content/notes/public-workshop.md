The site is moving away from a pure identity page and toward a small public
workshop: notes, field logs, technical experiments, and project context that can
accumulate without needing a full essay each time.

The goal is not a content machine. The goal is a place for useful fragments that
are easier to find here than in a chat log, issue thread, or local scratch file.
