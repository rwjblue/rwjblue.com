Most of my software work lives near the tools engineers use every day: build
systems, tests, CI, release workflows, and project infrastructure.

The interesting parts are usually not the glossy parts. They are compatibility,
debuggability, defaults, failure modes, and the tradeoffs that show up after a
tool has real users.
