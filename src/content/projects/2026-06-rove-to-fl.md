Driving the I-95 corridor from Rhode Island to Singer Island, FL with POTA
activations along the way. Arriving June 26 to participate in Field Day 2026
with a local club in the Palm Beach area.

The route passes through Connecticut, New York, New Jersey, Delaware, Maryland,
Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, and Florida — each state
with its own set of parks to hit.

Park selection and cluster planning are in progress.
